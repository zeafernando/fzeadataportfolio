McDonald's Big Mac Prices Over the World Data: Interactive Dashboard

The Tableau project aimed to visualize the price differences of McDonald's signature Big Mac across the world over the years. The project used a database containing information about the prices of Big Mac in various countries, available in both USD dollars and local currency.

The Tableau project utilized various visualization techniques to showcase the data, including maps, bar charts, and line graphs. The interactive features of Tableau allowed users to filter the data by country, year, and currency to analyze the trends in price differences over time.

The project revealed interesting insights, such as countries with the highest and lowest Big Mac prices, the impact of currency fluctuations on the prices, and how the prices have changed over the years.

BI Tool(s): Tableau.
